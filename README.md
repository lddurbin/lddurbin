## 🛠️ What I'm building

**Blue Dollars** — A platform hexping knowledge workers quantify the intangible value of their time. Co-founded with Liz Silvestrini.

**[Myndarin](https://www.myndarin.com/)** — A Mandarin learning app implementing the Hanzi Movie Method with SM-2 spaced repetition. Built because the existing tools weren't quite what I wanted while working through the Mandarin Blueprint course.

**[packagepal](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/packagepal/index.html)** — The goal of packagepal is to be a good pal for R package builders.

**[12 Days to Deming](https://deming.leedurbin.co.nz/)** — A respectful interactive rendering of an active-learning course based on the teachings of Dr. W. Edwards Deming, developed by Henry R. Neave.

## ✍️ What I'm writing

**[A History of Literature](https://historyofliterature.substack.com/)** — A Substack newsletter where I chronicle my reading journey, covering as many of the important works of literature as possible.

I've spoken at posit::conf(2024) in Seattle, run a 70+ member data visualisation Community of Practice, and once delivered a talk on Deming's Theory of Knowledge using Ibsen's *Pillars of the Community* as a live dramatic illustration. I think this combination is normal. Others disagree.
